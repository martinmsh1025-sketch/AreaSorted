# London Cleaning Platform - Website Pages v1

## Purpose

The website must do two jobs from day one:
- convert customers who want cleaning services
- build trust with job applicants coming from Indeed and other recruitment channels

The site should therefore be structured as both a sales website and a recruitment trust layer.

## Main Navigation

- Home
- Services
- Pricing
- Instant Quote
- Become a Cleaner
- About Us
- FAQ
- Contact

Secondary navigation / footer:
- Privacy Policy
- GDPR Policy
- Terms and Conditions
- Cookies Policy
- Cleaner Terms / Contractor Terms
- Careers / Recruitment
- London Area Pages

## SEO Content Priorities

Customer-facing keywords:
- domestic cleaning London
- regular cleaning London
- deep cleaning London
- house cleaner London
- cleaner near me London areas

Recruitment-facing keywords:
- self-employed cleaner jobs London
- domestic cleaner jobs London
- cleaner work London flexible hours
- part-time cleaner London self-employed

## Trust Signals Required Across Site

- company name and contact details
- policy pages in footer
- clear explanation of cleaner verification process
- secure payment messaging
- London service area references
- real onboarding steps for cleaners
- real support email / phone
- clear cancellation and reschedule rules

## Design Direction For v1

- must look like a real service business, not a generic template
- strong local London positioning
- clear split between customer CTA and cleaner CTA
- mobile-first forms
- fast loading pages for SEO and ad traffic
- structured service content for search engines
