This Cookie Policy explains how [LEGAL ENTITY NAME] trading as [PLATFORM NAME] ("Platform", "we", "us", "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, app, portals, and related online services.

1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use an online service. Similar technologies may also be used to store or access information on your device.

2. HOW WE CURRENTLY USE COOKIES

We currently use cookies and similar technologies to:
(a) keep the website and booking flow working properly;
(b) maintain secure login and session state where applicable;
(c) remember limited interface preferences;
(d) support security and fraud prevention; and
(e) where you choose to accept analytics cookies, understand how visitors use our website so we can improve booking journeys and site performance.

We do not currently enable advertising or marketing cookies through our main cookie banner.

3. COOKIE CATEGORIES WE CURRENTLY USE

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are required for the operation, security, and core functionality of our website or platform. For example, they may:
(a) keep you logged in;
(b) maintain session integrity during the booking process;
(c) support security checks, fraud prevention, and rate limiting; and
(d) remember essential technical state needed for core website features.

Because these cookies are necessary for the service you request, they may be used without consent where the law allows.

3.2 Analytics / Performance Cookies
If you choose "Accept analytics" in our cookie banner, we may load analytics technologies to understand how visitors use the website, such as which pages are viewed, how users move through the booking flow, and where improvements may be needed.

If you choose "Reject analytics" or "Only necessary", analytics cookies are not loaded.

3.3 Functional Preferences
We may store limited preference information connected with website behaviour or interface settings where needed for the user experience. Where a preference is strictly necessary for a feature you request, it may operate without separate consent.

4. COOKIE CONSENT

4.1 When you first visit our website, we show a cookie banner asking you to choose between:
(a) "Reject analytics";
(b) "Only necessary"; or
(c) "Accept analytics".

4.2 Strictly necessary cookies remain active regardless of your choice because they are required for login, security, booking, and essential site operation.

4.3 Analytics cookies are only loaded if you actively choose "Accept analytics".

4.4 Both "Reject analytics" and "Only necessary" keep non-essential analytics turned off.

4.5 After making a choice, you can reopen the cookie settings control shown on the site and change your preference at any time.

5. HOW WE STORE YOUR CHOICE

We store your cookie preference so we can remember it on future visits. This record may be stored using browser storage and/or a cookie for up to 12 months unless you clear it earlier through your browser or change your setting on the site.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Where analytics is accepted, third-party analytics providers may place or read cookies or receive usage data in accordance with their own service design.

We also use third-party technical services for payments, file handling, security challenges, maps, fonts, and infrastructure support. Not all of these services place non-essential cookies through the banner flow, but they may still process technical connection data when their features are used.

7. MANAGING COOKIES

You can usually control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect the operation, security, login state, or functionality of our website and booking process.

You can also change your analytics preference using the cookie settings control available on the site.
