# London Cleaning Platform - Wireframes v1

## Purpose

These are structural wireframes for the first three highest-priority pages:
- Home page
- Become a Cleaner page
- Instant Quote page

The aim is not final design polish. The aim is to make sure the most important content, trust signals, and conversion paths are in the right order.

## 2. Become a Cleaner Page Wireframe

### Header
- same site header

### Hero Section
Left side:
- headline: become a self-employed cleaner in London
- supporting copy focused on flexibility and reliable jobs
- bullet points:
  - flexible schedule
  - London-based work
  - online onboarding
  - fair scoring system

Right side:
- simple trust card:
  - secure application
  - company verification process
  - contract via DocuSign
  - support team contact

### How It Works
- step 1: complete your application
- step 2: upload ID, CV, photo, right-to-work documents
- step 3: review and contract signing
- step 4: set availability and receive jobs

### Who We Are Looking For
- experienced domestic cleaners
- reliable punctual workers
- London area coverage
- cleaners who can commit to accepted jobs

### What You Need
- ID
- CV
- photo
- right-to-work proof
- address proof
- DBS if available

### Self-Employed Model Section
- explain contractor model in simple language
- explain cleaner manages own tax obligations
- explain flexible acceptance of jobs

### Scoring Rules Summary
- start at 100
- completed jobs +10
- cancellation within 48 hours -20
- upheld complaint -30
- no-show -50
- under 60 placed at back of queue

### FAQ For Cleaners
- how often are jobs sent
- when do I get paid
- do I need my own supplies
- do I need DBS
- can I choose my hours

### Primary CTA Section
- button: Start Cleaner Application
- secondary CTA: Contact Recruitment Support

## 6. Mobile Priorities

- quote CTA visible early
- forms split into steps if too long
- sticky bottom CTA on key pages
- policy links accessible without clutter
- cleaner application uploads mobile-friendly

## 7. Conversion Priorities

Home page top goal:
- move visitors to quote or cleaner application

Become a Cleaner page top goal:
- build enough trust for applicant to start the form

Instant Quote page top goal:
- finish quote and continue to payment

## 8. Content Priorities

Always above the fold:
- what the business does
- where it operates
- why it is trustworthy
- the main action to take

Always before final CTA:
- simple explanation of process
- pricing clarity or expectations
- policy reassurance
- contact/support option
